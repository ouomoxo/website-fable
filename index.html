KATABASIS

A descent into the house of forgotten gods.

Scroll to descend

II — The Stair

Leave the light
at the door.

The stair was cut for slower centuries. Go down as they went down — one darkness at a time.

III — The Veiled

The gods did not die.
They were forgotten,
which is slower.

One figure remains, veiled in stone. The cloth has never been lifted, and it has never stopped falling.

IV — The Wing

What survives
is enough.

A single wing, recovered without its figure. Nothing else was found. Nothing else was needed.

V — The Winged One

One of them refused
to be forgotten.

She stands where the light comes down, wings open to a sky she cannot see. Look up.

VI — Anabasis

Go up.
Tell them the stone is still here.

KATABASIS is a digital monument. Nothing here was quarried; the marble and the dark are computed for whoever descends.
