## Light states
One key (front-left daylight), hemi fill, warm directional bounce, cool
rim, static contact decal. From p=0.93 the day turns late: key lowers,
cools, dims; fills fall; fog thickens. The sculpture never moves.

## Portrait (mobile)
Portrait aims the look 0.34 lower and widens fov +9°, framing the whole
tall monument high with the copy on the ground. Re-checked at 390×844.

## Forbidden
Orbit controls, handheld shake, dolly-zoom, moves through geometry, any
shot revealing the face, any motion of the figure itself.
